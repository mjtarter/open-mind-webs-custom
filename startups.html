Startups


A loose definition of startups: Entrepreneurial ventures that consist of newly created companies (sometimes just ideas) that are geared towards selling a product or service that seem to have a demand. Due to financial obligations and time constraints, a large amount of startups are formed by college students. In most cases these startups are working with very little capital & revenue and are facing high costs to expand and get the business off the ground.

I founded a startup when I was a student at Indiana University and faced these same problems. One of the main issues that was stopping my company from launching was that I needed a website but could not afford the prices web development companies were charging.
