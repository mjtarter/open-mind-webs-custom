How The Process Works


Planning / Strategy

There is no reason to feel overwhelmed at the initial stages of creating your website. We'll help you to find a starting point and take you through the process of coming up with the look, feel, and content structure that will work best for your site. With Open Mind Webs you do not need an entire vision of every single feature you want on your website. All you need is an idea and we can help you throughout the process of planning to completion.

Creation

One of the major benefits that Open Mind Webs offers is that we allow you to continuously make revisions to your site throughout the entire process. We realize that what looks good on paper, might not be what you had in mind once you see it on the screen. Whether it's using a different font or moving content around, we work with you throughout the entire creation process to ensure that you are satisfied with every single pixel of your site.

Maintenance

Once your website is completed and launched we don't just set you loose on your own. We offer maintenance and monthly content updates for free through the first 3 months and then offer plans to continue after that. You can continue to change content and make updates without ever having to mess with the code on the back end. However, if you'd prefer to take full control we can teach you how to make minor updates and changes yourself.
