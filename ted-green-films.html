Ted Green Films

tedgreenfilms.com

  • Web Development

An Indianapolis based film producer, Ted Green Films has been awarded 5 Regional Emmys for their documentaries. For this project the client designed the site and gave us rough sketches of the layout. We were also given the images, text, and videos to be used for the site. We then used this information to create a website that provides online marketing for their documentaries, recent news, previews, reviews, awards, bios, dvd purchases, and much more.
