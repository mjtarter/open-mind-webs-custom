Student Organizations


It is our mission to help every collegiate student organization, both big and small, heavily funded or strapped for cash, to fully unleash the powers of the web. In the technological world we live in today, having a website is vital in every aspect of your organization whether it be:
  • Recruiting members
  • Maintaining a convenient online interface to collaborate
  • Raising funds / Accepting donations
  • Planning Events
  • Sharing a cause / Spreading Awareness
  • Maintaining a professional appearance
  • Etc.
As a former Treasurer of the Indiana University Student United Way, I recognize the importance of student organizations and the impacts they can have on students, the university, and the community. I also understand the exponential influence these groups can have with the power of the web, but the challenges they face with finding a web development company and incurring the costs.

To assist student organizations, Open Mind Webs is offering our services at heavily discounted rates (as low as completely free for a select number of student orgs) that students can afford. We are also offering packages and software to allow students to be able to maintain and update their websites on their own for years to come.
