Who We Are

Open Mind Webs is a fast growing web development company founded in January of 2014. We provide a combination of top-notch web development services and cost effective prices that allow business both large and small to gain the competitive advantage necessary to succeed.

What We Do

We are a marketing and web services company that specializes in helping businesses market themselves through the web as well as manage their data. We develop first class websites and create database management systems with the newest technologies that comply with the latest web standards and best practices. Our motivation is to help you take full advantage of the web to market yourself or your business to be the most successful it can be.

Why Choose Us?

With a portfolio including a wide variety of web-based marketing services and clients ranging from optometrists to film producers to charitable organizations, we have the experience and expertise necessary to put you ahead of the competition.

We are also a relatively new organization focused on expanding our client base. During our current brand building campaign we are offering our services at discounted prices in order to raise awareness of our growing company. This combination of experience, expertise, and price reduction is the perfect formula to allow you to capitalize on our outstanding services.
